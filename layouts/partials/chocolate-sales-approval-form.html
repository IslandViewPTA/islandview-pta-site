Option 1: Chocolate Bar Sales

Island View kids LOVE to sell chocolate bars, and the fall soccer games and Oyster Run are prime selling times! Let’s kick off our school year with a spectacular fall fundraiser!

It's so easy – almost everyone says “Yes!” to a $1 chocolate bar!

How does it work?

Parents/Guardians must fill out and submit this form to Island View PTA by September 16, 2019. Chocolate boxes will go home with students on Thursday, September 19. Sell as many candy bars as you can by Friday, October 11!

Chocolate Sales Agreement between the parent and Island View PTA

Parents/Students will be held responsible for the retail value of any Lost, Damaged, Stolen Chocolate or Money.

Therefore:

  • Do not leave money/chocolate in desks, cubbies, under chairs, at home where little brothers/sisters/pets can get into it, or anywhere unsupervised.
  • Do not eat any chocolate unless it is paid for first.
  • Do not give chocolate to ANYONE unless they pay for it FIRST, including family and friends.
  • DO NOT store chocolate in freezer, refrigerator or car!
  • When selling chocolate, always do so with a parent/trusted adult present.

If you don’t think you’ll sell all of your chocolate, please contact the PTA by Monday, October 7. We will do our very best to sell all bars we check out, returning only the money ($60/box) by Friday, October 11.
